About MAL Ranker

How It Works

MAL Ranker uses the Elo rating system, the same algorithm used in chess rankings and competitive gaming. Instead of asking you to rate anime on a scale, we simply ask: "Which do you prefer?"

Through a series of head-to-head comparisons, the algorithm builds a precise ranking of your anime preferences. Each choice updates the ratings dynamically, converging on your true preferences over time.

Privacy

All processing happens in your browser. Your anime list is never uploaded to any server. The application runs entirely client-side.
