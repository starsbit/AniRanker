About MAL Ranker

psychology How It Works

MAL Ranker uses the Elo rating system, the same algorithm used in chess rankings and competitive gaming. Instead of asking you to rate anime on a scale, we simply ask: "Which do you prefer?"

Through a series of head-to-head comparisons, the algorithm builds a precise ranking of your anime preferences. Each choice updates the ratings dynamically, converging on your true preferences over time.

calculate The Algorithm Elo Rating Formula

Expected score: E = 1 / (1 + 10^((Rb-Ra)/400))

Rating update: R' = R + K × (S - E)

Where K=32 (sensitivity), S=1 for win, 0 for loss

Normalization

Final ratings are normalized to a 1-10 scale with a mean of 7.0, matching MyAnimeList's rating distribution. This ensures your ratings are compatible with MAL's system while reflecting your true preferences.

Comparison Optimization

We use an adaptive tournament-style selection to minimize comparisons. Target: ~N×log(N) comparisons for N anime, ensuring efficient ranking even for large lists.

privacy_tip Privacy

All processing happens in your browser. Your anime list is never uploaded to any server. The application runs entirely client-side.
